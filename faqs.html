FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. How do I log in to the Student Support portal?
Use your official email ID (example: name@stu.alliance.edu.in) and your password. After successful login, you will be redirected to the dashboard.
2. What should I do if I forget my password?
You should contact the university IT helpdesk or reset your password using official university support channels.
3. How can I check my attendance?
Go to the ACADEMICS section in the dashboard. You will find subject-wise attendance percentages displayed there.
4. Where can I get mental health or wellness support?
Visit the WELLNESS section where counselling, stress management, and health support services are available for students.
5. What should I do in case of an emergency on campus?
Immediately contact the campus emergency helpline or visit the nearest medical center for assistance.
6. How can I submit suggestions or feedback?
Go to the ACADEMICS section where a suggestion box is provided. Your feedback is sent to the admin console for review.
