FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. What is the admission process at Alliance University?
Admission is based on eligibility criteria, entrance examinations (if applicable), and personal interview. Students must apply through the official university portal and submit required academic documents.
2. What are the fee payment options available?
Fees can be paid online through the university portal using net banking, UPI, debit/credit cards, or through demand draft as per university guidelines.
3. When are the semester examinations conducted?
Semester examinations are generally conducted twice a year at the end of each academic semester, as per the academic calendar published by the university.
4. How can students apply for hostel accommodation?
Students can apply for hostel accommodation during admission or through the student portal. Allocation is based on availability and eligibility criteria.
5. What is the attendance requirement to appear for exams?
Students are generally required to maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each course to be eligible for semester examinations.
6. How are internal marks calculated?
Internal marks are based on assignments, quizzes, attendance, presentations, and continuous evaluation conducted throughout the semester.
7. How can students contact the university for queries?
Students can contact the university through official email (enquiry@alliance.edu.in) or call the university helpline provided on the official website.
8. What documents are required during admission?
Students must submit marksheets, transfer certificate, identity proof, passport-size photographs, and other documents specified in the admission guidelines.
